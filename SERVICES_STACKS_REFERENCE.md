## Campaign Packages & Tier Logic

### Package Structure Explained

Each package has **3 service tiers**:

1. **Core Campaign Stack (Monthly Retainers)**
   - Must-have services that run entire campaign
   - Examples: Messaging, Media, Volunteer Management, Voter Data
   - Why locked in: You can't run a campaign without messaging or voter contact
   - Typical: 6-8 services bundled

2. **Launch Work (One-Time Setup)**
   - Foundation-building that happens at campaign start
   - Examples: Website, Branding, Logo, Donation Platform
   - Why locked in: You do this once, then it's attached
   - Typical: 2-4 services, $2-5k total

3. **Flexible Add-Ons (Optional Monthly)**
   - Services that can be scaled, swapped, or removed
   - Examples: Polling, Debate Prep, Video Production, Extra Ad Spend
   - Why flexible: You adjust based on campaign momentum
   - Typical: 3-6 services, $1-3k additional/month
